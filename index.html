about

This special, small batch cider is a collaboration between Lieb Cellars and Woodside Orchards. Dry, crisp and aromatic, it’s a cider made by winemakers for wine lovers.

Grown and made on the shores of the Long Island Sound, Sound Cider Co. celebrates life by the sea, in the country and under the stars.
